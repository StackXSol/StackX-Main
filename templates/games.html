Dodge Bar

Dodge Bar is an endless Hyper casual game where you play as a Bar and your aim is to tackle all the obstacles coming your way. Sounds easy right ? But it isn't that simple. Your bar constantly keeps rotating and you can either guide it to left or right side, apart from this the obstacles too are unpredictable.

PixStellar

Pixstellar is a fun Space Shooter Hyper casual game where you play as captain of a XTR12 Spaceship. Your spaceship is small but fast and capable of getting powerful upgrades along your journey. From Multi Canons to Destruction Aura, you can keep annihilating your enemy as long as you are agile and show good anticipation.
